THE VISION BEHIND

Hey there! We're Alice and Janina - craft is at the heart of what we do.

Since 2018, Janina has hosted 20+ creative workshops across Europe, bringing people together through hands-on making. Alice has built relationships with 60+ artisans across the Maghreb through her brand Faridique.

This project was born from a simple idea: what if people could experience craftsmanship at its source - working side by side with artisans?

So we created exactly that: The Creative Artisan Experience - an intimate series of workshops in Tunis led by master artisans. Join us.

- Alice & Janina
